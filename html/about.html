About the B.Tech Department

Sharing its boundary with International Cricket Stadium, having a clear view of majestic Dhauladhars and dark rain-rich Monsoon clouds, gurgling streams and towering White Mountains, Government Post Graduate College, Dharamshala, the college of excellence, carries a long magnificent history of its own and the institution is now ninty seven years old. The foundation stone of the college was laid by Sir Louis Dane, the then Lt. Governor of Punjab on 4th May, 1912. It was the first college in the state to introduce the faculties of Arts, Science and Commerce and also Post Graduation in different subjects. The college is also accredited by National Assessment and Accreditation Council, Bangloroo (NAAC). The collective efforts of teaching and non-teaching staff have already brought splendid and brilliant results and the roots of the college are spread throughout the country.

Welcome to the Department of B.Tech at Govt. PG College Dharamshala. We are committed to excellence in engineering, education and research.

    Our mission is to:

  • Provide high-quality education in various fields of technology and engineering.
  • Conduct cutting-edge research and contribute to technological advancements.
  • Foster innovation and creativity in our students.

Key Information:

Location:

Our campus is located in Dharamshala, Himachal Pradesh, offering a vibrant and conducive environment for learning and research.

Accreditation:

GOVT PG COLLEGE DHARAMSHALA is accredited by NAAC as grade 'B++', ensuring the highest standards of education and facilities.

Courses:

Our department houses a diverse range of engineering disciplines, including Computer Science and Masters in Computer Applications.

Specializations:

We offer specialized B.Tech programs in areas such as Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science.

Duration:

Our B.Tech program spans four years, structured across eight semesters, and the MCA program spans two years, structured across four semesters.

Admission:

Admission to our B.Tech programs is highly competitive and is based on merit, including performance in HPSET and JEE examinations and academic achievements.

What Sets Us Apart:

World-Class Faculty:

Our experienced faculty members are experts in their respective fields, providing students with top-notch guidance and mentorship.

Cutting-Edge Facilities:

Our B.Tech Department is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories, libraries, and research facilities, empowering students with hands-on learning opportunities.

Research Opportunities:

We encourage and facilitate research projects and internships, enabling students to explore innovative solutions to real-world problems.

Student Organizations:

Engage with like-minded peers through our student chapters of leading engineering organizations and various engineering clubs.

Career Opportunities:

Our strong industry connections and dedicated placement cell ensure that students have access to excellent job opportunities after graduation.

Extracurricular Activities:

Beyond academics, our students actively participate in sports, cultural events, and technical fests, creating a well-rounded college experience.

Alumni Network:

Join our thriving alumni network, which provides valuable career guidance and networking opportunities.

Financial Aid:

We offer scholarships and financial aid options to eligible students, making quality education accessible to all.

Global Reach:

Govt. PG College Dharamshala has international collaborations, offering opportunities for student exchange programs and joint research ventures.

We invite you to explore our website for more in-depth information about our BTech department and the exciting opportunities it offers. Join us in shaping the future of engineering and technology !!
